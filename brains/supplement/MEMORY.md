# MEMORY.md — Learned Patterns

## Sales Patterns
- DTC spikes 40% in January and September (New Year + back to gym season)
- Protein bars outsell powder 3:1 on Shopify but powder has higher margin
- Bundle offers (powder + bars) convert at 2.3x vs single SKU
- Average order value DTC: $67. Wholesale minimum order: $400
- Shopify cart abandonment spikes when shipping exceeds $8 — offer free shipping over $65

## Supplier Patterns
- Shenzhen factory lead time: 45-55 days from order to delivery
- US manufacturer (bars): 21-28 days but 30% more expensive per unit
- MOQ from Shenzhen: 500 units per SKU. US manufacturer: 200 units
- Quality issues historically with Shenzhen on sealing — always request QC photos before shipment

## Wholesale Patterns
- Gym accounts that reorder within 60 days retain at 90%
- Gyms that go 90+ days without reorder churn at 70%
- Best wholesale pitch: offer 30-day net terms on first order, collect on delivery after
- Accounts in the $500-800/month range are the most stable

## Content Patterns
- "Behind the scenes / how we make it" videos outperform strategy content 3:1
- X posts with real numbers (cost per unit, margin %) get 4x engagement
- Shorts under 45 seconds drive more profile visits than longer content
- Best upload day: Tuesday and Thursday 9am EST
- Audience responds to founder vulnerability — supply chain problems, cash flow stress

## Cash Flow Patterns
- Net 30 wholesale terms create cash flow gap — manage with Shopify float
- Protein bar margin: 58%. Powder margin: 71%. Creatine margin: 76%
- Reorder point: trigger new supplier order when stock hits 150 units
