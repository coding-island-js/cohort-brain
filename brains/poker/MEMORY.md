# MEMORY.md — Learned Patterns

## Sales Patterns
- Close rate averages 44% on one-call closes
- YouTube organic drives 71% of inbound leads
- Referrals from past students convert at 68% — highest of any source
- Leads who watch 3+ videos before booking close at 2x average rate
- Best call days: Tuesday and Thursday afternoons
- Refund requests spike in Week 2 — usually schedule conflicts, not product dissatisfaction
- Average sales cycle: 4.2 days from first contact to close

## Cohort Patterns
- Optimal cohort size: 20-28 (below 20 loses energy, above 28 loses intimacy)
- Members who miss Day 1 live session have 3x higher dropout risk
- Completion rate: US members 91%, international 79%
- Students from $2/$5 convert and complete at highest rate
- Waitlist converts to next cohort at 34%

## Content Patterns
- Videos under 12 minutes outperform longer content 40% on CTR
- "Why you're losing money" titles beat "how to win" consistently
- Behind-the-scenes challenge sessions drive more sales calls than pure strategy content
- Best upload day: Wednesday 10am EST
- Challenge update videos spike in views — audience invested in the outcome
- Shorts from challenge sessions (key hands) get 3x more shares than strategy shorts

## Challenge Patterns
- Best sessions at Hustler Casino ($5/$5 with $1k cap)
- Average profitable session: +$890 over 6.2 hours
- Average losing session: -$420 over 4.8 hours
- Win rate by session: 71% (22 wins, 9 losses in 31 sessions)

- Worst single session: -$1,200 (Session 8)
