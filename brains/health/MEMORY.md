# MEMORY.md — Learned Patterns

## Sales Patterns
- Free consult converts to membership at 38%
- Highest converting source: X posts with real lab result breakdowns
- Referrals from existing members convert at 71%
- Price objections almost always come from Foundation tier — upgrade conversation often resolves it
- Members who complete onboarding labs within 14 days retain at 94%
- Members who delay labs beyond 30 days churn at 3x average rate

## Retention Patterns
- Average member tenure: 14 months
- Churn triggers: price (40%), relocation (25%), "feeling better, don't need it" (20%), other (15%)
- Re-engagement works: a personal check-in from the physician recovers 60% of at-risk members
- Elite members churn at half the rate of Foundation members
- Members with a named strength coach churn at half the rate of those without

## Care Team Patterns
- Dr. Patel handles all initial consultations — 90 min each
- Optimal patient load per physician: 40 members
- Dietitian and strength coach can each handle 35 members effectively
- Hiring lead time for physicians: 6-8 weeks from job post to start
- Best hire source: physician networks and health optimization communities

## Content Patterns
- Lab result breakdowns on X drive 3x more consult bookings than generic health tips
- Longevity content outperforms fitness content on X for our audience
- Case studies (anonymized member transformations) convert best
- Best post time: Monday and Wednesday 8am EST
- Newsletter open rate: 41% — above industry average
